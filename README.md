## **Production Cost and Profitability Analysis**


##**Insights**
1. Raw material cost contributes the highest portion of total production cost, indicating that better supplier negotiation or bulk purchasing can reduce expenses.
2. Certain products like LED Panel 40W generate lower profit, suggesting a need for pricing or cost optimization.
3. The average profit per unit is ₹14.31, showing moderate profitability with scope for improvement through cost control.
4. Cost and profit trends show fluctuations, indicating inconsistent production or demand patterns.
5. Available stock levels are high (~21K units), suggesting potential overproduction or slow-moving inventory.
Recommendations

- Reduce raw material costs through better sourcing or bulk purchasing.
- Review low-performing products and adjust pricing or discontinue them.
- Improve labour and machine efficiency.
- Optimize inventory to reduce excess stock and holding costs.


##**Problem Statement**
The company faces high production costs and needs to improve profitability. This project focuses on analyzing cost components, product performance, and inventory efficiency using data analytics.
Dataset Description
The dataset includes product name, manufacturing and expiry dates, raw material cost, labour cost, machine cost, selling price, quantity, and available stock.
